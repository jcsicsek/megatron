What is tabb.io

Tabb.io a next-generation open platform for retail credit. Our cloud-based system enables small and midsize merchants to easily offer private label credit to their customers. Our open platform lets developers access the credit functionality they need to create innovative products, while enabling small and midsize banks to put their capital to work in the private label credit niche.

Why work with tabb.io?

  • Our team combines experience from the finance, retail, and marketing industries, so we understand the needs of both merchants and lenders.
  • We have a passion for enabling smaller merchants to better compete with “big box” retailers.
  • We give merchants control of their end-to-end customer experience through co-brand and white label lending products.

Merchants can increase their sales and strengthen customer loyalty by offering deferred payments, monthly installment plans, and revolving lines of credit under their brand. Tabb.io makes this possible all with one simple integration.
