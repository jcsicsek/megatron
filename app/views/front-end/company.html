Meet the tabb.io Team

The tabb.io team comes from a diverse background in payments, ad tech, e-commerce, financial business process management, and more.

Ben Brown

Chief Executive Officer

Ben spent 6 years as a payments strategy consultant for First Annapolis Consulting where he worked with clients worldwide on strategy, operations, product, and mergers and acquisitions. Ben has Dual B.S. degrees from the University of Maryland, College Park.

Frank Fumarola

Chief Operating Officer

Frank came from ad tech and ecommerce where he was VP of Product for 50onRed. He managed product managers, designers, user experience engineers, and front-end developers. Frank has Dual B.S. degrees from the University of Maryland, College Park.

Jeff Csicsek

Chief Architect

Jeff came from Big Human where he was a Sr. Software Engineer. He developed many of their clients prototypes and MVPs. He previously worked for an energy sustainability web company and an IT consultancy. Jeff has a B.S. from Virginia Tech.
