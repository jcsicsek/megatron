What is tabb.io

Tabb.io is an open lending platform that enables your company to offer private label credit programs to your customers. You can increase brand loyalty and conversion rate through deferred payments, monthly installment plans, and revolving lines of credit for your brand. All with one simple integration.

Why work with tabb.io?

  • We're a team with vast experience in ecommerce and payments.
  • We have a passion for enabling smaller merchants to better compete with the big box stores.
  • We don't want our brand in your product experience.

WildAttire and it's main properties, Ties.com and Scarves.com, are very concerned with brand loyalty and controlling the user experience. That's why we chose tabb.io versus other branded solutions. We've seen about 10% of our customers utilizing our deferred payments and we have plans to test installment plans next!

COO & President of WildAttire, Omar Sayyed
