Deferred Payments

Tabb.io enables merchants to offer short-term credit to consumers so they can buy now and pay later. In-store or online, merchants can use deferred payment offers to convince consumers to buy today. Online, deferred payment can also address the concerns of cautious shoppers who want to receive their goods before they pay.

Installment Loans

Merchants can offer purchase-specific financing plans with tabb.io. Installment plans let consumers pay for their purchases in equal, fixed installments over 3 to 24 months. Installment plans are simpler to offer than revolving credit and may be appropriate in a wider variety of situations.

Revolving Credit

Merchants can use tabb.io to issue private label credit accounts. Recurring customers can put all their purchases on one store account. Private label credit expands consumers’ purchasing power and reduces merchants’ payments cost. Tabb.io supports accounts tied to a virtual account or plastic card.
