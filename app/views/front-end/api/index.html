Getting Started

These technical documents and API guides will walk you through the tabb.io implementation and customization as ewll as show you how your customers will experience the product. In a nutshell, the experience will follow the below flow:

  • Your customer will browse your site and choose a product or service that they're interested in.
  • They will then proceed to checkout and choose your stores credit option as a payment method.
  • Once your customer clicks the checkout button, you will collect a few pieces of information that will enable tabb.io to verify identity and assess credit risk. You will pass this information along with the order information over to tabb.io.
  • Tabb.io will respond with a refusal or an acceptance with credit terms based on the information sent over.
  • The customer now has store credit with your store, along with any benefits you enabled such as deferred payment!
